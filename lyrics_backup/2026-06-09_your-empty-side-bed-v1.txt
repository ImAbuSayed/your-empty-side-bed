[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the shadows begin to fall
I hear the echo when you call
I want to lay where the moonlight is shed
Right in the warmth of your empty side bed

[Verse 1]
The room is quiet, the hour is late
I see the hunger, I feel the weight
I brush a fever across your skin
I want to know where the night begins

[Pre-Chorus]
I pull you closer and cross the line
I want to hold you and make you mine
The world is fading and slipping from view
I only want to be tangled with you

[Chorus]
I want to fill up your empty side bed
I want to lay where your pillows are spread
I want to whisper the things left unsaid
Lost in the heat of your empty side bed

[Whispered] (Yeah, right in your empty side bed)
